intro:
Introduction for 蟾蜍狗Toadog

(x)d (anything):
    Roll an Icosahedron (20 sided dice) x times to test your luck!
    *x must be less than 10 ><
    *d(anything) rolls a dice as default!

d char (name) (anything)*N:
    Command for creating a board game character's stat.
    After insertion for the character's name, leave space between every element.
    Each element would be rolled by a 20 sided dice by default.
